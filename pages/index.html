About Me

My deep dive into software began with C++, where I learned that the 'magic' of high-level languages is built on fundamental principles of memory and logic. This foundation allows me to deconstruct complex problems and learn new technologies rapidly, seeing the patterns that connect them all.

I put this foundation into practice at Cotton SP, where I led a small team, designed database models with Django, and honed my skills in creating pragmatic, maintainable code. Now, I am channeling that experience into modern back-end development with Go and Python.

Outside of code, I'm a musician and a cook, but it's my passion for factory games that best describes my engineering mindset. I love the process of iterating, optimizing complex systems, and planning for scale. I always keep a pen and paper by my side to structure my thoughts.
